Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Pop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Overview

Pop is a camera app designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe you should be able to use our app without any concerns about your personal information.

Data We Don't Collect

To be completely clear, Pop does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Camera footage or images
  • Any data that could identify you personally

Camera Access

Pop requires access to your camera to function. This access is used solely to display the live viewfinder and apply the color isolation effect in real-time.

Important: Your camera feed is processed entirely on your device. No images or video are ever transmitted to any server or third party. The camera data stays on your phone.

Photo Library

When you capture a photo or video in Pop, it is saved directly to your device's photo library. We do not upload, sync, or transmit your photos or videos anywhere. They remain entirely under your control.

No Account Required

Pop does not require you to create an account or sign in. All features are available immediately after download with no registration needed.

Offline Functionality

Pop works completely offline. No internet connection is required to use any feature of the app. This means no data is transmitted from your device while using the app.

No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs in Pop that collect or transmit your data.

Children's Privacy

Pop does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date above. However, given our commitment to not collecting data, we do not anticipate any significant changes.
