Quick Tips

Point at colorful scenes

Pop works best when there are multiple colors in view. The contrast between your selected color and the grayscale background creates a dramatic effect.

Use flash in low light

Color detection works best with good lighting. If you're in a dim environment, turn on the flash to help Pop identify colors more accurately.

Try video mode

Video is great for scanning large areas. Hold the capture button to record while you slowly pan across a room to find all objects of a specific color.
